## The world doesn't need another productivity app.

Seriously. There are 47,000 to-do list apps. Another project management tool won't change anyone's life. You know what will? An app that moans when you slap your laptop.

We're not kidding. Someone made $5K in 3 days doing exactly that.

## The StupidApps Manifesto

We believe in:

- **Fun over function** — Life is too short for boring software
- **Shipping over perfecting** — A stupid app that exists beats a perfect app that doesn't
- **Virality over marketing** — If your app makes someone laugh, they'll share it for free
- **Impulse buys over subscriptions** — $5 for a laugh? No brainer. $15/month for productivity? Let me think about it...

## Why stupid works

The most shared content on the internet isn't useful — it's entertaining. The same applies to apps.

When someone downloads Slap-O-Meter and shows it to their friend, that friend downloads it too. No marketing budget required. No SEO strategy. No content calendar.

Just pure, unfiltered stupidity.

## What's next?

We're building a whole collection of apps that shouldn't exist. Each one more unnecessary than the last. And we're having the time of our lives doing it.

**Stay stupid, friends.**
